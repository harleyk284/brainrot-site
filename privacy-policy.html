Privacy Policy

Escape the Brainrot

Effective Date: February 7, 2026  |  Last Updated: February 7, 2026

Brainrot Studios ("we," "us," or "our") develops and publishes Escape the Brainrot (the "Game"), a mobile application available on Android and iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our Game.

By downloading, installing, or using the Game, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Display Name: A username you choose, visible to other players on leaderboards and friend lists.
  • Friend Connections: When you add friends using a Player ID or display name, we store that relationship.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, graphics hardware, and system memory. This helps us optimize performance and diagnose crashes.
  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous device-based identifiers used for account creation and authentication. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or physical address.
  • Gameplay Data: Scores, distance traveled, coins and gems earned, power-ups used, missions completed, characters unlocked, items equipped, and session timestamps. This data is used to save your progress and populate leaderboards.
  • Purchase Information: Records of in-app purchases (product IDs, timestamps). Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play); we do not collect or store credit card numbers or billing addresses.

1.3 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Our Game uses the following third-party services, each of which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob Google LLC Advertising (rewarded videos, interstitials, banners) Device identifiers, approximate location, ad interaction data, app usage patterns
PlayFab Microsoft Corporation Authentication, cloud saves, leaderboards, friend system Anonymous account ID, gameplay statistics, friend relationships, display name

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Save and sync your game progress across devices via cloud saves.
  • Provide leaderboards and social features so you can compete and connect with friends.
  • Display advertisements to support the free-to-play model. If you consent to personalized ads, ad content may be tailored to your interests. You can opt out at any time.
  • Process in-app purchases and deliver purchased content (gems, skins, subscriptions).
  • Diagnose crashes and improve stability using crash reports.
  • Improve the Game by analyzing aggregated, anonymized gameplay patterns.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse in purchases and leaderboards.

3. Your Choices and Consent

When you first launch the Game, you are presented with a consent screen where you can choose to enable or disable:

  • Personalized Ads: Controls whether ads are tailored to your interests. You can deny this and still see non-personalized ads.
  • Crash Reporting: Controls whether crash data is sent to Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Cloud Saves: Controls whether your progress is synced to PlayFab servers. If disabled, progress is saved locally only.

You can update your preferences at any time through the Game's Settings menu.

3.1 Ad Tracking (iOS)

On iOS devices, we request your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before collecting the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) for ad personalization. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

3.2 Remove Ads

You may purchase the "No Ads" option to permanently remove all advertisements from the Game.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the third-party services listed in Section 1.3, and only for the purposes described. Specifically:

  • Google AdMob receives device and usage data to serve advertisements.
  • PlayFab (Microsoft) receives gameplay data to provide cloud saves, leaderboards, and social features.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google) receives device and crash data to help us fix bugs.

Your display name, Player ID, high score, player level, and approximate last-login time are visible to other players who add you as a friend.

5. Data Retention

  • Gameplay data is retained for the lifetime of your account or until you request deletion.
  • Crash reports are retained for 90 days by Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Ad interaction data is retained by Google according to their data retention policies.
  • Local data (on your device) is deleted when you uninstall the Game.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted between the Game and our servers uses encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).
  • PlayFab cloud data is stored on Microsoft Azure infrastructure with enterprise-grade security.
  • We do not store passwords, payment details, or sensitive personal information on our servers.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email below and we will delete such information promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data. You can reset your consent and local data through the Game's Settings. For server-side data deletion, contact us at the email below.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Opt-Out: Withdraw consent for optional data collection (personalized ads, crash reporting, cloud saves) at any time through the in-game Settings.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format.

8.1 For EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

The legal basis for processing your data is your consent (provided through the in-game consent screen) and our legitimate interest in operating and improving the Game. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before withdrawal.

8.2 For California Residents (CCPA)

You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States (Google, Microsoft/PlayFab). These transfers are necessary to provide the Game's services and are protected by appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the Game or by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
